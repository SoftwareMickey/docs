## Deployment Process

Follow these steps to get your Node.js application live on VectorIHub.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Repository">
    Upload or connect your GitHub/GitLab repository. VectorIHub scans your codebase for framework-specific configs.

    <Tip>Ensure your `package.json` is in the root directory for automatic detection.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Environment Setup">
    VectorIHub automatically:
    * Detects `package.json`
    * Installs dependencies via `npm`, `yarn`, or `pnpm`.
    * Uses the Node.js version specified in your `engines` field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go Live">
    VectorIHub builds and starts your app inside a secure container, exposing the correct port automatically.

    <Check>Deployment usually takes less than 60 seconds.</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
