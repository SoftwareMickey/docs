# Release v0.1.0 <Badge text="Public Beta" color="green" />

Welcome to the very first VectorIHub changelog! We are officially opening our doors to beta testers to help us refine the future of **Push-to-Deploy** infrastructure.

---

##  New Features

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Auto-Dockerization" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" color="#16A34A">
    Our core engine now supports automatic detection for **Node.js, React, and Go**. Launch without writing a single Dockerfile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GitHub Native Integration" icon="github" color="#000000">
    Securely connect your account and deploy any repository **public** or **private** with a single click.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Infrastructure Upgrades

We've overhauled our backend to ensure your applications stay fast and secure from the moment they are provisioned.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Runtime">
    **VHB Cloud Runtime** has been optimized for high-density container orchestration, significantly reducing cold start times for serverless-style deployments.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Security">
    **Automatic SSL:** Every deployment now receives an automated Let's Encrypt SSL certificate. Security is no longer an afterthought; it‚Äôs the default.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Networking">
    **Custom Subdomains:** Beta users can now claim and manage their own `*.vectorihub.run` namespaces directly from the dashboard.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
