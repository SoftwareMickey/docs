## How Stack Detection Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload or Connect Repository">
    Push your code or link your GitHub repository to VectorIHub.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Framework Detection">
    VectorIHub scans project files like package.json, go.mod, and build configs to accurately identify your stack.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Auto Dockerization & Build">
    A production-grade Dockerfile is generated automatically based on your detected framework and runtime.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Instant Deployment">
    Your container is deployed with a live endpoint in seconds on secure, isolated infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
