# Welcome to VectorIHub

VectorIHub is an automated deployment platform designed to bridge the gap between development and production. By automating the entire DevOps lifecycle, we allow you to focus on **code**, while we handle the **containers**.



<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Quickstart" icon="rocket" href="/quickstart">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why VectorIHub?

Managing infrastructure is a distraction. VectorIHub eliminates the "it works on my machine" problem by standardizing your environment automatically.

<Columns>
  <Column>
  ### Automate Everything
  Manual Dockerfiles are a thing of the past. Our engine analyzes your stack and builds optimized images on the fly.
  </Column>
  <Column>
  ### Scale Effortlessly
  Go from a side project to a production-grade cluster without needing a dedicated DevOps team.
  </Column>
</Columns>

<Note>
VectorIHub currently supports Node.js, Python, Go, and Rust with zero configuration. More languages are added weekly!
</Note>

## Core Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Auto-Dockerization">
    <Tip>No more `COPY . .` or `EXPOSE` debugging.</Tip>
    Our platform detects your language, version, and dependencies to generate a secure, lightweight container environment.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Instant Deployment">
    Push your code via CLI or GitHub integration. We handle the orchestration, SSL, and routing instantly.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Continuous Updates">
    Your live environment stays in sync with your `main` branch. We utilize rolling updates to ensure zero downtime.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Getting Started

Follow these steps to go live today.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Repository">
    Link your GitHub or GitLab account to VectorIHub.
    <Check>Permissions are scoped only to the repositories you select.</Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Environment">
    Add your `.env` variables directly in the dashboard.
    <Warning>Never commit your secrets to your source code!</Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Click **Deploy** and watch the logs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to know Docker?">
    No! While we use Docker under the hood, VectorIHub handles the creation and management of containers for you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a custom Dockerfile if I have one?">
    Absolutely. If a `Dockerfile` is present in your root directory, our engine will respect your custom configuration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
