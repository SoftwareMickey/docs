VectorIHub is officially out! This first release introduces a powerful auto-dockerization and deployment platform that allows developers to instantly containerize and host their apps, streamlining development and deployment workflows.

With VectorIHub, developers can deploy projects without worrying about complex infrastructure setup. Just upload your code, and VectorIHub handles the containerization, building, and hosting automatically. This release also sets the stage for collaborative networking, real-time updates, and seamless scaling in future versions.
