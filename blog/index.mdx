### How it Works

Stop worrying about complex infrastructure. Our pipeline handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on building.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Code">
    Push your repository or upload your project files directly to the VectorIHub dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Build">
    Our engine detects your environment and handles the **containerization** and **building** process automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scale & Host">
    Your app is hosted on our optimized infrastructure with seamless scaling and real-time updates.
