Welcome to VectorIHub! This guide will walk you through the process of containerizing and deploying your first AI application.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

* A VectorIHub account (Sign up at [vectorihub.com](https://vectorihub.com))
* A GitHub repository with your application code
* A basic configuration file (optional, but recommended)

---

## 3 Steps to Launch

Follow these steps to get your app live on our global infrastructure.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Repository">
    Log in to the [VectorIHub Dashboard](https://vectorihub.com) and click **Your Project**. Select **deploy repo**.
    
    <Info>
      VectorIHub automatically detects your language (Python, Node.js, Go) and prepares the environment.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your Environment">
    Upload your environment variables if any are required by your application.
    
    <CodeGroup>
    ```yaml .env (Example)
    PORT: 5173
    DB-URL: https://example.database.com
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy & Monitor">
    Click **"Deploy"**. Our engine will start the **Auto-Dockerization** process.
    
    <Check>
      Your app will be live at `https://your-app.vectorihub.run` once the build completes!
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
